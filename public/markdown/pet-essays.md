# 应用文

## A postcard describing an interesting museum you visited
- 一张描述你参观过的有趣博物馆的明信片

**正文**：

Hi Alex,

I'm having a great time in London! Yesterday, I visited the Natural History Museum, and it was amazing. The dinosaur exhibition was the best part. I saw a huge Tyrannosaurus rex skeleton, and it was much bigger than I had imagined. I also enjoyed the Earth Hall, where you can experience an earthquake simulation. It felt so real! The building itself is beautiful too. I took lots of photos to show you.

Hope you're well. See you soon!

Best,
Chris

- 嗨，亚历克斯，
- 我在伦敦玩得很开心！昨天，我参观了自然历史博物馆，简直太棒了。
- 恐龙展是其中最精彩的部分。我看到了巨大的霸王龙骨架，比我想象的要大得多。我也很喜欢地球馆，那里可以体验模拟地震。感觉非常真实！博物馆建筑本身也很漂亮。我拍了很多照片给你看。
- 希望你一切都好。很快再见！
- 最好的祝愿，
- 克里斯

# 记叙文/短文

## An article explaining how students can help protect the environment
- 一篇解释学生如何帮助保护环境的文章

**正文**：

**Our Planet, Our Future**

Protecting the environment is easier than you think, and we students can make a big difference. First, we can reduce waste at school. Simple actions like using both sides of our paper and carrying a reusable water bottle instead of buying plastic ones are very effective.

Secondly, we should save energy. Remember to turn off lights and computers when we leave a classroom. At home, we can encourage our families to do the same. Finally, getting involved in local clean-up projects or starting a school recycling club can be a great way to contribute. Let's work together for a greener world!

- **我们的星球，我们的未来**
- 保护环境比你想象的要容易，我们学生可以发挥重要作用。首先，我们可以在学校减少浪费。一些简单的行动，比如使用纸张的正反面，以及携带可重复使用的水瓶而不是购买塑料瓶，都非常有效。
- 其次，我们应该节约能源。当我们离开教室时，记得关掉电灯和电脑。在家里，我们可以鼓励家人也这样做。最后，参与当地的清洁项目或创办一个学校回收俱乐部，将是做出贡献的好方法。让我们为创造一个更绿色的世界共同努力吧！
